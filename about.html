FROM THE DEVELOPERS

This project was the initiative of four members of the CS Association of Nepal, a student organization whose underlying philosophy is to use computer science to solve the real-world issues.


We consider it a great honor. It is a rare opportunity for us to use our skills and knowledge for such a noble cause. We strongly believe that this is a milestone for us. The CS Association family expresses their deepest gratitude towards Blind Youth Association Nepal for their belief and support.
