HOW CAN YOU HELP?

Helping in general is a very noble act. It shows your kind and compassionate nature. But, helping people with disabilities in their educational endeavors and journey is a much greater deed. If you want to be a part of this initiative and experience the treserous feeling that comes with helping others, we would love to express our warm welcome. There are two options for you: voluntary help or help via a small payment. We highly encourage you to provide voluntary help, and if you prefer the latter option, we urge you to offer assistance in exchange for the least payment.

To connect with help seekers, click on the link below. This will ask you for minor details about you and the area where you can offer your assistance.
